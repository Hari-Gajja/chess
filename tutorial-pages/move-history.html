Tracking Moves

The move history panels show:

  • White moves on the left panel
  • Black moves on the right panel
  • Move numbers and notations
  • Special moves indicators

Chess Notation

Understanding move notation:

  • K = King, Q = Queen, R = Rook
  • B = Bishop, N = Knight
  • x = Capture, + = Check
  • # = Checkmate, O-O = Castling
