How to Make Moves

Moving Pieces

To move a piece:

  1. Click and hold the piece you want to move
  2. Valid moves will be highlighted on the board
  3. Drag the piece to any highlighted square
  4. Release to complete the move

Special Moves

  • Castling: King moves two squares towards a rook
  • En Passant: Special pawn capture
  • Pawn Promotion: Pawns reaching the opposite end
