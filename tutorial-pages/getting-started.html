Getting Started with Chess

Starting a New Game

To begin playing chess:

  1. Locate the "Start Game" button at the top of the board
  2. Click to initialize a new game
  3. The board will automatically set up with pieces in their starting positions
  4. The timer will begin at 10:00 minutes for each player

Initial Board Setup

Understanding the initial position:

  • White pieces are positioned at the bottom
  • Each player starts with 16 pieces
  • The first row contains the major pieces
  • The second row contains eight pawns
