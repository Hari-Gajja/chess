Forsyth–Edwards Notation (FEN)

Understanding FEN

FEN is a standard notation for describing chess positions:

rnbqkbnr/pppppppp/8/8/8/8/PPPPPPPP/RNBQKBNR w KQkq - 0 1
  • Piece Placement: Each rank is described from 8th to 1st
  • Uppercase: White pieces (K,Q,R,B,N,P)
  • Lowercase: Black pieces (k,q,r,b,n,p)
  • Numbers: Count of empty squares

FEN Components

  1. Piece placement (from white's perspective)
  2. Active color (w/b)
  3. Castling availability (KQkq)
  4. En passant target square
  5. Halfmove clock
  6. Fullmove number
